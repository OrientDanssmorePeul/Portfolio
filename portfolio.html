About Me:

Hi, my name is Orient. I’m an 18-year-old Kadazan living in George Town, Penang, Malaysia. Even though I was born in Sabah, Penang has become my home, with its mix of vibrant culture, heritage streets, and modern life. I’m a game developer, always creating small projects and dreaming of building games that share stories from Malaysian culture, especially my Kadazan roots. When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me playing chess—either online or with friends at local cafés—or listening to music, which helps me relax and focus. I also have a huge love for turtles. They’ve always fascinated me for their calmness and strength, and I often spend time visiting turtle conservation centers or beaches around Penang to learn more about protecting them. Life here is a balance for me: I’m staying connected to my heritage, enjoying city life, and chasing my dreams in game development, one step at a time.

My Favourites

Chess

Chess is a timeless board game where two players go head-to-head in a battle of minds. Every move counts as you plan ahead, outsmart your opponent, and protect your king.

Terraria

Terraria is a 2D sandbox game where you explore, build, fight, and survive in a world full of surprises. From digging deep underground to battling massive bosses and crafting powerful gear, there’s always something new to do.

Phonk

Phonk is a music genre that blends old-school hip-hop, Memphis rap samples, and smooth lo-fi beats. With its gritty sound and eerie atmosphere, it’s perfect for late-night drives or getting in the zone.

Chicken Chop

Chicken chop is a delicious dish made with a juicy, tender piece of grilled or fried chicken, usually served with black pepper or mushroom sauce. It's often paired with fries, coleslaw, or baked beans.
